## Overview

The Support department manages customer interactions -- triaging support tickets, maintaining a knowledge base, and tracking customer satisfaction through NPS surveys. It helps a solo founder provide professional customer support at scale.

## Quick Actions

| Action | What It Does |
|--------|-------------|
| **Open tickets** | Show all open support tickets prioritized by urgency |
| **Write KB article** | Create a knowledge base article for a topic |
| **NPS survey** | Analyze NPS results with score breakdown and themes |

## Example Prompts

- "Show all open support tickets prioritized by urgency."
- "Write a knowledge base article about how to set up API authentication."
- "Analyze recent NPS survey results -- what are the recurring themes?"
- "Draft a response to this customer complaint: [paste details]."
- "What are the top 5 most common support requests this month?"

## Capabilities

- **Ticket** -- support ticket management with auto-triage and priority assignment
- **Knowledge** -- knowledge base article creation and maintenance
- **NPS** -- Net Promoter Score tracking and analysis
