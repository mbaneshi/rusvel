## Overview

The Harvest department finds freelance opportunities, scores them for fit, and drafts proposals. It scans sources like Upwork, LinkedIn, and GitHub, then manages a pipeline from discovery through to proposal submission.

Powered by the Harvest engine with its discover > score > qualify > propose > track pipeline.

## Quick Actions

| Action | What It Does |
|--------|-------------|
| **Scan opportunities** | Search for new gigs across configured sources |
| **Score pipeline** | AI-score all opportunities by fit, budget, and probability |
| **Draft proposal** | Generate a tailored proposal for a specific opportunity |

## Example Prompts

- "Scan for new Rust freelance opportunities on Upwork."
- "Score all opportunities in the pipeline and rank by fit."
- "Draft a proposal for this Upwork gig: [paste URL or description]."
- "What opportunities have the highest close probability this week?"
- "Archive all opportunities older than 30 days with no activity."

## Pipeline Stages

Opportunities flow through: **Cold** > **Contacted** > **Qualified** > **ProposalSent** > **Won** / **Lost**

Each opportunity tracks source, score, estimated value, and metadata.
