## Overview

The Product department handles product strategy -- maintaining roadmaps, prioritizing features, analyzing user feedback, and optimizing pricing. It helps a solo founder make data-driven product decisions.

## Quick Actions

| Action | What It Does |
|--------|-------------|
| **View roadmap** | Show features, milestones, and priorities |
| **Add feature** | Add a new feature to the roadmap |
| **Pricing analysis** | Analyze pricing tiers and suggest optimizations |

## Example Prompts

- "Show the current product roadmap with milestones and deadlines."
- "Add 'Dark mode support' as a medium-priority feature for Q2."
- "Analyze our pricing tiers -- are we leaving money on the table?"
- "Summarize user feedback from the last 30 days. What patterns emerge?"
- "Compare our feature set against competitor X."

## Capabilities

- **Roadmap** -- feature tracking with priorities, milestones, and deadlines
- **Pricing** -- tier analysis, willingness-to-pay modeling
- **Feedback** -- user feedback aggregation and pattern detection
