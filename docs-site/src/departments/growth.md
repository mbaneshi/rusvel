
## Overview

The Growth department focuses on metrics and growth levers -- conversion funnels, cohort retention analysis, KPI tracking, and churn prediction. It turns raw data into actionable growth insights.

## Quick Actions

| Action | What It Does |
|--------|-------------|
| **Funnel analysis** | Analyze conversion rates with drop-off recommendations |
| **KPI dashboard** | Show MRR, DAU, churn rate, and growth metrics |
| **Cohort report** | Generate retention report with weekly/monthly trends |

## Example Prompts

- "Analyze the signup-to-paid conversion funnel. Where is the biggest drop-off?"
- "Show the KPI dashboard with MRR, DAU, churn rate, and week-over-week growth."
- "Generate a cohort retention report for users who signed up in January."
- "What is our predicted churn rate for next month based on current trends?"
- "Recommend 3 experiments to improve activation rate."

## Capabilities

- **Funnel** -- conversion tracking and drop-off analysis
- **Cohort** -- user group retention analysis over time
- **KPI** -- key performance indicator dashboards and trend tracking
