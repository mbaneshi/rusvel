
## Overview

The Finance department manages your financial picture -- income and expenses, profit and loss reports, cash runway forecasting, and tax optimization. It provides the financial intelligence a solo founder needs to make informed decisions.

## Quick Actions

| Action | What It Does |
|--------|-------------|
| **Record income** | Log a new revenue transaction |
| **Log expense** | Record a business expense |
| **Calculate runway** | Forecast how long your cash will last |
| **P&L report** | Generate a profit and loss breakdown by category |

## Example Prompts

- "Record $5,000 income from Acme Corp for March consulting."
- "Log a $49/month expense for hosting on Railway."
- "Calculate my runway based on current burn rate."
- "Generate a P&L report for Q1 2026."
- "What are my top 3 expense categories this month?"

## Capabilities

- **Ledger** -- double-entry transaction tracking
- **Tax** -- tax category tagging and optimization suggestions
- **Runway** -- cash flow forecasting based on burn rate and income trends
