
## Overview

The Distribution (Distro) department manages how your product reaches users -- marketplace listings, SEO performance, affiliate programs, and partnership channels. It ensures your product is discoverable and well-positioned across all distribution channels.

## Quick Actions

| Action | What It Does |
|--------|-------------|
| **SEO audit** | Check keyword rankings and page performance |
| **Marketplace listings** | Review listings with status, downloads, and revenue |
| **Distribution strategy** | Analyze channels and recommend new ones |

## Example Prompts

- "Run an SEO audit on our landing page. What keywords should we target?"
- "Review all marketplace listings -- which ones are underperforming?"
- "Suggest 3 new distribution channels for a developer tools product."
- "Draft a marketplace listing for our Rust library on crates.io."
- "Set up an affiliate program structure with commission tiers."

## Capabilities

- **Marketplace** -- listing management across app stores and package registries
- **SEO** -- keyword tracking, page speed, and search ranking optimization
- **Affiliate** -- partner program management and commission tracking
