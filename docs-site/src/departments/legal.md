
## Overview

The Legal department handles the legal side of running a business -- drafting contracts, ensuring GDPR compliance, managing intellectual property, and maintaining terms of service and privacy policies. Essential for a solo founder who cannot afford dedicated legal counsel for every decision.

## Quick Actions

| Action | What It Does |
|--------|-------------|
| **Draft contract** | Generate a contract for a specific engagement |
| **Compliance check** | Audit GDPR and privacy policy compliance |
| **IP review** | Review intellectual property assets and protections |

## Example Prompts

- "Draft a freelance services agreement for a 3-month Rust consulting engagement."
- "Run a GDPR compliance check on our SaaS application."
- "Review our open-source licensing -- are we compliant with all dependencies?"
- "Generate a privacy policy for a SaaS product that processes user data."
- "What IP protections should I have in place before launching?"

## Capabilities

- **Contract** -- drafting and reviewing service agreements, NDAs, and terms
- **Compliance** -- GDPR, privacy, and regulatory compliance auditing
- **IP** -- intellectual property tracking and protection strategy
